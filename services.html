Your Questions, Answered

How is SEO for coaches different from other industries?

Coaching and therapy fall under Google’s "Your Money Your Life" (YMYL) categories. This means Google requires higher levels of trust, authority, and safety. We focus on ethical, safe strategies that respect client privacy while building your authority.

How long before I see SEO results for my coaching business?

SEO is a long-term play, but for local visibility, we often see Google Business Profile improvements in 4–8 weeks. Full organic keyword rankings typically take 3–6 months to stabilize.

Do you also build funnels or just do SEO?

We do both. An SEO strategy brings people to your site, and a high-converting funnel (like those we build in GoHighLevel) ensures those visitors actually book a call with you.

Which tools do you use?

We specialize in GoHighLevel for CRMs/Funnels and n8n for advanced technical automations. For SEO, we use industry leaders like SEMrush, Ahrefs, and Google Search Console.

Can you work with my existing website platform?

Yes. Whether you use WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, or a custom GHL site, we can optimize the technical and on-page elements to improve your visibility.
